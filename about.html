About CG and Sons Co.

Mission Statement

At CG and Sons Co, our primary objective as a young player in the food franchise industry is to establish a scalable and sustainable business that caters to Filipinos through high-quality food services under the esteemed brands of our franchise partners.

As a franchise company, CG and Sons Co is committed to continuous growth and expansion. We actively seek like-minded partners in this competitive industry who share our vision for a sustainable long-term future. Our focus is on creating high-quality products and providing efficient service that ensures customer satisfaction.
